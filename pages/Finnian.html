About

Abigail Reilly lives in Hagerstown, Maryland with her husband Matthew and their daughter, Regean. She has always had an appreciation for nature and the beauty of all of God's many creations, but she feels a special affinity towards plants and flowers. She first became interested in photography in high school when she took a photography class and absolutely loved it. Ever since then she has enjoyed capturing the beautiful scenes of nature in her photos.

When Abigail isn't outside enjoying nature or taking pictures, she enjoys reading, completing puzzles, and playing board games with friends and family. She also treats her neighbors to ukelele music from time to time, when she remembers to practice. Abigail's passion for life inspires those around her.
