Ty

As a changeling, Ty can take many different forms. Though he's picked up a few personas during his travels the main ones are Alia, a human bard (pictured above); Al, a half-elf diplomat; Alex, an angry tiefling; and Allison, a drow spy. Ty does have a true form, but that's... not for you to see. Ty's detached and somewhat impulsive personality remains consistent across his various personas. His favorite pastime is pulling animals from his grey bag of tricks (though playing dragonchess is a close second).
